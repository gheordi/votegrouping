Gomataon Eco Lodge - live with us !

Gomataon is a typical traditional Waorani community. We want to valorise the way our ancestors were living, to keep the langage, our identity and customs, our culture, old tales, myths and legends. We want to protect or territor, the wildlife, like our ancestors did.
